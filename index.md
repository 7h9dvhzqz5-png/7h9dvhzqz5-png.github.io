# Privacy Policy for StackRush Impossible

Last updated: 09/04/26

## Overview

StackRush Impossible is a mobile game designed for entertainment.

We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how data is used.

---

## Data Collection

StackRush Impossible does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details.

However, the app uses third-party services (such as advertising networks) that may collect certain data automatically.

This may include:
- Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
- App interaction data (e.g. gameplay activity)
- Basic device and usage information

---

## Advertising and Tracking

StackRush Impossible displays advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners.

These partners may collect and use data to:
- Show relevant ads
- Measure ad performance
- Improve their services

This may involve tracking across apps and websites, in accordance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

You will be asked for permission before any tracking occurs.

---

## Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services, including but not limited to:
- Advertising networks (e.g. Google AdMob, Unity Ads, or similar)

These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

## Data Usage

Collected data may be used for:
- Displaying advertisements

- Analysing gameplay behaviour (in aggregate)

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Data may be shared with third-party partners only as required to:

## Data Storage

We do not store personal data on our own servers.

Any data collected is handled by third-party services as described above.

## Your Choices
